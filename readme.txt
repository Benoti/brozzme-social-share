Embed social sharing system within your WorPress site.

== Description ==
Embed Social Share in your WorPress site recursively or with one shortcode.
An admin settings page allow you to choose between many options.

Options available in the settings page :
- enable / disable automatic social share embed in posts and pages
- enable / disable your own box title
- enable / disable fontawesome icons
- look and hover styles
- enable / disable hover.css effects
- choose your social network


== Installation ==
1. Upload taengo-social-share to the \"/wp-content/plugins/\" directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the \"Plugins\" menu in WordPress.
3. Share it if you are happy


== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= What is the shortcode to display the social share bar ? =
Just add [brozzme-social-share] where you want in your post.

= What happen if I have shortcode in my post and that I want to activate the automatic insertion ? =
You don\'t need to worry, your shortcode will be deactivate before the insertion.
